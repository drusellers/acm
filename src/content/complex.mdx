# We build complex systems

A modern business process requires complex systems, but they needn't be complicated.

<Definition term="Complex">
    A system that is composed of mulitple other systems
</Definition>

<Definition term="Complicated">
    consisting of many interconnecting parts or elements; intricate

New people always want to ask "Why" when they encounter a new system that doesn't match their mindset. I'm no different, I want to understand your processes down to the core. I want to understand how your sales process, impacts your End of Month close. I want to understand how your billing processes, impact your software development processes.
